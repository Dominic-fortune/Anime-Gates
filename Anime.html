Plunderer

Plunderer is a new series to the anime community and has made a nice first impression on viewers. The concept of this anime is that everyone has a count and depending on who you are it goes up and down based on certian actions you do. If your count gets to 0 then you are dragged into the underworld where everyone is assumed to die. Its a interesting show and I will be checking in to see what happens throughout the story.
